Video Games Sales Analysis

his project analyzes video game sales trends over the years, focusing on platform performance, regional sales, and genre popularity. The dataset includes historical sales data for video games across multiple platforms.

Project Overview

  • Dataset: Video Game Sales.
  • Tools Used: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn.
  • Key Metrics: Global Sales, Regional Sales (NA, EU, JP, Other), Platform Performance, Genre Popularity.
  • Visualizations: Bar charts, Pie charts, Line graphs, Heatmaps.

Key Insights

  • Identified the top-selling platforms and genres globally.
  • Analyzed regional sales trends (North America, Europe, Japan, Others).
  • Visualized the correlation between critic scores and sales.
  • Explored the decline of certain platforms over time.
